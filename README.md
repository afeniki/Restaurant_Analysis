**RESTAURANT ANALYSIS DASHBOARD**

A Data Visualization and Insights Project

**Overview**

This project presents a comprehensive Restaurant Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI.
The dashboard examines restaurant distribution, cuisine categories, customer demographics, and overall performance ratings across different states and cities.
It provides insights that can support decision-making for restaurant owners, analysts, and investors.

**Objectives**

Understand the distribution of restaurants by cuisine, state, franchise type, and area
Analyze customer demographics including smoking preference, drinking level, marital status, occupation, budget, and transportation
Evaluate restaurant performance using food, service, and overall ratings
Identify top-performing restaurants and areas for improvement

Dashboard Sections
**1. Restaurant Distribution**

Key findings include the following
San Luis Potosi has the highest number of restaurants representing 64.62 percent of the total
Most restaurants are not franchise owned with 108 out of 130 classified as independent
The most common cuisine types are Mexican, Cafe and Bakery, Fast Food, and American

**2. Restaurant Performance Ratings**

The dashboard evaluates service rating, food rating, and overall rating across all restaurants
Top performing restaurants based on rating totals include
Cafeteria Y Restaurant El Pacifico
La Cantina Restaurant
Tortas Locas Hipocampo

These restaurants display consistently high performance across food, service, and overall ratings

**3. State Level Analysis**

Distribution of restaurants by state
San Luis Potosi 64.62 percent
Morelos 17.69 percent
Tamaulipas 17.69 percent

San Luis Potosi represents the dominant region suggesting higher population density, commercial activity, or culinary demand

**4. Customer Demographics**

The dashboard includes several demographic categories

*Smoking Preference*
Most consumers are non smokers totaling 78.99 percent

*Drinking Level*
The largest segment consists of social drinkers accounting for 36.96 percent
Abstainers and casual drinkers follow

*Occupation*
The majority of customers are employed representing 81.88 percent
Students and unemployed groups make up the minority

*Marital Status*
Most customers are single representing 89.86 percent

*Budget Range*
Medium budget consumers form the largest group at 65.94 percent
High budget customers account for only a small portion

*Transportation Method*
Public transportation is the most common method of travel with 59.42 percent
Cars and walking follow as secondary methods

**Insights and Recommendations**
*Business Insights*

San Luis Potosi presents the strongest market opportunity
Independent restaurants dominate creating potential for franchise growth
Top performing restaurants can serve as operational benchmarks
Medium budget customers form the core consumer base
High usage of public transportation suggests restaurants should be positioned in accessible locations

**Recommendations**

Prioritize marketing and expansion efforts in San Luis Potosi
Study operational strategies of high performing restaurants to improve service in low performing locations
Develop promotions targeting working adults and social drinkers
Consider delivery partnerships due to the high number of public transport users
Introduce premium menu items to capture the high budget segment




The Restaurant Analysis Dashboard offers a detailed and data driven view of restaurant performance, customer behavior, and market distribution.
These findings can support strategic planning and operational decisions within the food service sector.
